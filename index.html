How to Use Kick & Dunk Co.

  • Home: This is where you can discover our latest products and deals. Look for the 'Home' option in the navigation bar to return to the starting page at any time.
  • Shopping: If you're looking to purchase jerseys, find the 'Shopping' section in the navigation bar. This is where our catalog of products is available for you to browse through.
  • About Us: Curious about who we are? The 'About Us' section tells you the story of Kick & Dunk Co., our values, and our journey.
  • Contact: Should you have any questions or need support, the 'Contact' section is where you can find ways to reach out to us.
  • Help: For guidance on how to use the site, the 'Help' section, which is what you're reading now, will provide all the necessary information.
  • Account: By accessing your personal account, you can manage your orders, set your preferences, and update your personal details.
  • Search: If you are looking for something specific, use the search functionality by clicking on the search icon or pressing 'S' on your keyboard.
  • Cart: The items you have added to your shopping cart can be reviewed anytime by navigating to the 'Cart' section.

Creating an Account

To enhance your shopping experience, consider signing up for an account. This allows you to make purchases, track your orders, and receive updates. You can sign up or log in by selecting the user icon typically located at the top right of the page.

Making a Purchase

Ready to buy? Simply browse our selections in the 'Shopping' section. Once you've found your desired jersey, select the product to view the details. Choose your size and then add the item to your cart. When you're ready to complete your purchase, proceed to review your cart and then check out to finalize your order.

Product Information

For every product listed in our 'Shopping' section, you will find detailed information including price, available sizes, materials, and care instructions. Simply click on the product image or name to access this information and make an informed purchase.

Managing Your Account

Your account dashboard is the place to manage all aspects of your relationship with us. Here you can update your personal information, change your password, review past orders, and manage your subscription preferences.

Tracking Your Order

Once you have made a purchase, you can track the progress of your order from processing to shipment. Details regarding tracking will be provided via email after your purchase, and you can also access this information in the 'Account' section under 'Order History'.

Technical Issues

If you encounter any technical issues while using our website, please reach out to us immediately through the 'Contact' section. Provide a detailed description of the problem so our technical team can address it promptly.

Feedback and Suggestions

We welcome your feedback and suggestions on how we can improve your experience. Please use the feedback form available in the 'Contact' section to share your thoughts with us.

Privacy and Security

Your privacy and the security of your personal information are paramount to us. Please visit our Privacy Policy section to understand how we protect and use your data.
